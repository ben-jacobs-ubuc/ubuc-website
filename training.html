Training

UBUC offers a wide veriety of training for all levels of diver. Whether you are a dive leader (or equivalent) with more than 400 dives or somone who just wants to try something - we have something for you. From basic diving to speciality skills, you can learn this all through us.

Non-BSAC Divers

Whatever agency you trained with, you are more than welcome to join and come diving with us. All divers who trained with other agencies will have to attend a workshop to get to know the BSAC decompression tables and get a Statement of Alternative Training (SALT) sticker signed by the Diving Officer and stuck in your logbook.

Ocean Diver

The following 'entry level' qualifications are the equivalent to BSAC Ocean Diver:

  • CMAS One Star
  • NASDS Open Water
  • NASDS Advanced Open Water
  • NAUI Scuba Diver
  • NAUI Advanced Scuba Diver
  • PADI Open Water
  • PADI Advanced Open Water
  • Royal Navy Ships Diver
  • SAA Open Water
  • SSI Open Water
  • SSI Advanced Open Water

Sports Diver

The following qualifications are the equivalent to BSAC Sports Diver:

  • CMAS Two Star
  • HSE Commercial Diver:
    • HSE Scuba Diver (Part 4)
    • HSE Surface Supply Diver (Part 3)
    • HSE Closed Bell Diver (Part 2)
    • HSE Surface Supply Top Up Diver (Part 1)
  • NASDS Rescue Diver
  • NAUI Scuba Rescue Diver
  • PADI Rescue Diver
  • SAA Club Dive
  • SSAC Third Class Diver
  • SSI Advanced Open Water Diver with 'Stress and Rescue' Speciality
  • Army Compressed Air Diver

Dive Leader

The following qualifications are equivalent to BSAC Dive Leader:

  • NASDS Dive Supervisor
  • NAUI Divemaster
  • PADI Divemaster
  • SAA Dive Leader
  • SSAC Second Class Diver
  • SSI Dive Control Specialist

Advanced Diver

The following qualifications are equivalent to BSAC Advanced Diver:

  • CMAS Three Star Diver
  • SAA Dive Supervisor

Continuing Your Diver Training

There will be plenty of opportunities for you to continue your training, whatever your current qualification. We will be running Skill Development Courses throughout the year. There will also be lots of diving trips so check the facebook group regularly.

If you are an experienced diver Instructor Training is an option, with divers of Sports Diver standard and above able to start with the BSAC Instructor Foundation Course and assist with pool training.

If you have any questions contact the Training Officer or the Diving Officer.


New & Existing BSAC Divers

Training for beginners begins every Autumn term, training you to be an Ocean Diver. Training consists of weekly classroom lectures and pool training. Once you've completed these and a theory test you may progress to open water diving, usually by attending our annual Porthkerris trip in late March. After 5 open water dives you gain your qualification and may dive with other fellow Ocean Divers!

If you have a BSAC qualification you can join the club and come diving with us at any time. We hope you to continue your training with us and progress to the next qualification. We encourage everyone to get up to the level of Sports Diver within their first year. This opens up all the trips especially the summer expeditions.
