Trip Resources

When organising a trip the club has a very simple procedure list to make sure the trip is a success:

  1. Decide on the destination, type of diving and the level the dives will be suitable for
  2. Check with the diving officer, equipment officer and boats officer (if necessary)
  3. Email the club (include: when, where, approximate cost, suitable level, etc...)
  4. Ensure there is an advanced diver or a dive leader as necessary on the trip, then organise instructors (if needed), transport and dive rota
  5. Email the people going on the trip with the meeting time and who is in which car
  6. Discuss with the equipment officer what kit you need and when you need it
  7. Email the DO with the names of the people on the trip, so that they can register trip with the Safety Office in the union
  8. Check weather – if it's bad you may need to cancel or change your plans
  9. Organise the route and ensure all drivers have a copy
  10. Final equipment check (top up bottles, spares, O2 kit etc), check tide times and final check with advanced diver

When you get back:

  1. Contact Diving Officer to confirm all went well
  2. Collect the money (remembering any for the club and to split petrol money evenly)
  3. Write the trip report and email to club or add it to the wiki yourself
